Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to armanckeser.com ("the Website"). I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit my Website.

2. Data Collection

2.1 Information You Provide

I don't collect any personal information unless you voluntarily provide it, such as when you contact me through the Website.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Website, I collect anonymous usage information through Umami Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool. This information includes:

  • Pages visited
  • Referral sources
  • Time spent on the Website
  • Browser type and device information
  • Country of origin (based on IP address, which is not stored)

Importantly, Umami Analytics:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not track users across websites
  • Does not collect or store personal identifying information
  • Does not sell data to third parties

3. Purpose of Data Collection

I use analytics data solely to:

  • Understand how visitors interact with the Website
  • Identify popular content
  • Improve the Website's user experience
  • Monitor Website performance

4. Data Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The anonymous analytics data is accessible only to me.

5. Data Security

I implement reasonable security measures to protect any information collected. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, I cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Links

The Website may contain links to third-party websites. I have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites or services.

7. Children's Privacy

The Website is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Since minimal personal data is collected, most rights will not apply. However, you may:

  • Opt out of analytics by using browser extensions or features that block scripts
  • Request information about any data I may have collected about you

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective when posted.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me through the contact information provided on the Website.
